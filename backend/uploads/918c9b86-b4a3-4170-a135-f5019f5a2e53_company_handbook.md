# Company Overview
CORPWISE is a cutting-edge AI assistant designed to bridge the gap between your employees and your corporate knowledge base. It uses Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to allow you to chat with internal documents—HR policies, technical guides, and project archives—just as you would with a colleague. Key features include hybrid search, source citations, and enterprise-grade security.

# Work Hours & Availability
Core Hours: Employees must be available between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM local time to facilitate collaboration.
Remote Work: A "Hybrid 3/2" model is in effect (3 days in office, 2 days remote).
Absence Reporting: Report sick leave via the WorkDay portal by 9:00 AM on the day of absence.
