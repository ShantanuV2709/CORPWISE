# Human Resources Policy

## Attendance & Remote Work
- **Core Hours**: Employees must be available between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM local time.
- **Remote Work**: A "Hybrid 3/2" model is in effect (3 days in office, 2 days remote).
- **Absence Reporting**: Report sick leave via the WorkDay portal by 9:00 AM.

## Data Privacy & Conduct
- **Confidentiality**: All internal documents must be labeled "Internal Use Only".
- **Data Handling**: Customer PII (Personally Identifiable Information) must never be stored on local devices.
- **Code of Conduct**: Zero tolerance for harassment. Report issues anonymously to `ethics@corpwise.internal`.

## AI Usage Policy
- AI-generated insights are advisory and should not replace managerial judgment.
- Do not paste sensitive customer passwords into any AI tool, including Corpwise.
